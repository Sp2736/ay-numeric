Meet Our Experts

YUSUF TINWALA

Founder & Director

Strategic Vision & Operations

Yusuf is a results-driven Chartered Accountant with over 18 years of International experience across the UK, Qatar, and Oman. He specializes in establishing robust financial policies and end-to-end reporting frameworks that drive business growth.

An entrepreneur at heart, Yusuf successfully established a UK-based Accounting Firm. He mastered the complexities of UK Accounting, Management Reporting, Tax Planning and Business Services and ensuring that our technical solutions are always built on a foundation of financial accuracy.

ZAINAB MARCHAWALA

Operations & Director

Compliance & Financial Governance

Zainab is a Qualified CS and Accounts manager with over 5 years of dedicated experience, bridging the gap between complex legal governance and day-to-day accounting excellence.

She possesses an in-depth mastery of UK direct and indirect tax legislation, ensuring that our clients remain fully compliant with HMRC requirements. Her expertise spans precision payroll management, pension auto-enrolment, and the preparation of statutory accounts under UK GAAP.
